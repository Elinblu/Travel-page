Why Oslo?

Oslo, the fjord-side capital

Oslo, the fjord-side capital, is a city where urban life and raw nature blend seamlessly. Nestled between the Oslofjord and vast forests, the city offers both vibrant cultural experiences and peaceful escapes within minutes of each other. Whether you're exploring modern architecture in Bjørvika, taking a ferry to the islands, or hiking in the surrounding hills, Oslo invites you to slow down and enjoy the balance between sea, city, and silence.

From its Viking roots to its contemporary design scene, Oslo embraces both history and innovation. Museums like the MUNCH, the Fram Museum, and the Nobel Peace Center reflect the city’s deep cultural heritage, while buzzing neighborhoods like Grünerløkka offer trendy cafés, street art, and a creative pulse. In Oslo, tradition and progress walk hand in hand—always with the fjord in sight.
